The datasets used in this study include the Microsoft Research Wiki-QA corpus, SQuAD 2.0, NewsQA, and Natural Questions. All datasets are publicly available. The Wiki-QA corpus and NewsQA can be accessed from the official Microsoft website at https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/download/details.aspx?id=52419 and https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/project/newsqa-dataset/ respectively, while SQuAD 2.0 is available at https://rajpurkar.github.io/SQuAD-explorer/, and Natural Questions at  https://ai.google.com/research/NaturalQuestions
